Kenny Peak

I use the name Kenny Peak to refer to the high point on Session's Ridge just above Kenny Creek Cabin (a burnt down pile of rubble as it was burned down several years ago.) The south-west fast of this peak has a lot of great ski terrain, but the (solar) aspect is not favorable, and winds typically hammer this area pretty badly. Still, my best powder was in this area. I love to go up to this peak, do several laps off it, then a final one that goes all the way down to a stream just below the cabin, skin back to the cabin, then ski back out the way I came. The best approach is gas pipeline, which, under good conditions, allows you to ski all the way back down to the bottom of the canyon where you can ski all the way back to your car.
